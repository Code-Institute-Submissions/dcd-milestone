About Us

Hi there, I'm Mel! I designed and built the Constant Cookbook as a project in Software Development. I'm a food lover and I hope you enjoy using this website to find the snack your heart desires!

Whether you want to share your creations with the world or you're looking for some inspiration for tonight's dinner, the Constant Cookbook is here to help!

Other websites to get you cooking!

Here at the Constant Cookbook, we want everyone to find the dish to make their day - check out these other great websites and food bloggers for inspiration to make some top-notch meals!

BBC Good Food - BBC Good Food is about "good recipes, and about quality home cooking that everyone can enjoy."
Simply Recipes - Simply Recipes is a family cooking and food blog with hundreds of healthy, whole-food recipes for the home cook.
The Sprouted Kitchen - The Sprouted Kitchen uses food to "slow my mind down by focusing on one task and also, for relationships - to gather people around a table."
Not Without Salt - Ashley's goal is to is help you find pleasure, joy and sustenance in good food.
My New Roots - My New Roots wants to help you discover a whole new world of flavour and nourishment.
